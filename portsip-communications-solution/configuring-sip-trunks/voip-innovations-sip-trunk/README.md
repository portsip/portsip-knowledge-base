# VoIP Innovations SIP Trunk

[VoIP Innovations](https://carrierservices.sangoma.com/) provides a comprehensive portfolio of carrier-grade voice services and CPaaS solutions designed for developers, business customers, and enterprise deployments.

PortSIP PBX natively integrates with [VoIP Innovations SIP trunking](https://carrierservices.sangoma.com/) to support calling and SMS/MMS messaging. Within the PortSIP PBX configuration, users can select VoIP Innovations as their service provider to enable and fully utilize both voice and messaging services.

To successfully integrate VoIP Innovations with PortSIP PBX, you must complete the following high-level steps:

1. **Obtain a VoIP Innovations Account**\
   Create an account with VoIP Innovations to gain access to SIP trunking and messaging services.
2. **Log In and Configure VoIP Innovations Settings**\
   After account creation, sign in to the VoIP Innovations portal and complete the required configurations for voice and SMS/MMS services.
3. **Configure PortSIP PBX Integration**\
   Set up the VoIP Innovations SIP trunk and messaging integration within the PortSIP PBX Web Portal.
